Board and Training Packages

Full Service Board
Full care board includes a 12x12 stall, daily stall cleaning, twice daily feeding of grain and supplements and hay, daily turn out (as weather permits). All stall beeding, hay, and grain is included in your board
Partial Board
Partial board includes a 12x12 stall, twice daily feeding of grain and supplements and hay, and dailty turnout (as weather permits). All stall stall bedding, hay, and grain is included in your board. With partial board you are responsible for cleaning your own stalls at least once a day. Partial boeards who fail to keep their stalls clean will have their stall cleaned by our staff and will incur additoonal changes
Pasture Board
Horses on pasture board have free roam to 20 acers of pasture and have access to several run-in shelters. Grain, hay, and water is provided as part of your pasture board. Blanketing is available for an additional charge, as needed
